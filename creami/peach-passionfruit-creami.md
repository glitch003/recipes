# Peach Passionfruit Creami

Bright, tropical, and tangy — a lighter creami that lets the fruit do the talking.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen peach slices | 250 g |
| Frozen passionfruit cubes | 100 g |
| Whole milk | 120 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 80 g |
| Granulated sugar | 50 g |
| Corn syrup (or honey) | 15 g |
| Xanthan gum | 0.5 g |

**Total:** ~615 g

## Instructions

1. **Blend the base:** Add milk, cream, sugar, corn syrup, and xanthan gum to a blender. Blend until the sugar is fully dissolved and the xanthan gum is incorporated, about 30 seconds.
2. **Add fruit:** Add peach slices and passionfruit cubes. Pulse a few times to break up large chunks, then blend until mostly smooth — a few small pieces are fine and add nice texture.
3. **Pour and freeze:** Pour into a Ninja Creami pint container, leaving about 1 cm of headspace. Snap on the lid and freeze for at least 24 hours until completely solid.
4. **Process:** Remove the lid and place the pint in the Ninja Creami. Run the **Sorbet** cycle first. If it's not creamy enough, re-spin on the **Ice Cream** cycle.

## Notes

- Passionfruit is intense, so a little goes a long way.
- Corn syrup helps keep the texture scoopable by lowering the freezing point.
