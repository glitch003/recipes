# Marion Blackberry Banana Creami

Rich, creamy, and deeply fruity — banana builds the body while blackberry brings the flavor.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frozen marion blackberries | 150 g |
| Frozen banana slices | 150 g |
| Heavy whipping cream | 100 g |
| Whole milk | 80 g |
| Granulated sugar | 45 g |
| Corn syrup (or honey) | 15 g |
| Xanthan gum | 0.5 g |
| Vanilla extract | 2 g |

**Total:** ~542 g

## Instructions

1. **Blend the base:** Add cream, milk, sugar, corn syrup, xanthan gum, and vanilla extract to a blender. Blend until smooth and the sugar is dissolved, about 30 seconds.
2. **Add fruit:** Add blackberries and banana slices. Blend until smooth — banana will emulsify into the base. A few small blackberry seed bits are expected and add nice texture.
3. **Pour and freeze:** Pour into a Ninja Creami pint container, leaving about 1 cm of headspace. Freeze for at least 24 hours until completely solid.
4. **Process:** Remove the lid and run the **Ice Cream** cycle. The banana makes this one process very smoothly — one pass should be enough. Re-spin if needed.

## Notes

- Marion blackberries have more complexity than regular blackberries — they're worth featuring as the star.
- A pinch of salt helps brighten the berry flavor.
- The banana adds natural pectin and creaminess, so this one processes beautifully.
