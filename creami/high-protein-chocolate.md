# High Protein Chocolate Creami (with 63% chips)

## Ingredients (grams)

- 380 g milk (2% recommended; adjust as needed)
- 60 g heavy whipping cream
- 40 g unflavored whey protein powder
- 45 g 63% cocoa chocolate chips
- 10 g sugar
- 1.0 g xanthan gum
- 1 g salt (tiny pinch, but important)
- Optional: 2–3 g vanilla extract (makes “chocolate” taste more chocolatey)

## Instructions

1. **Melt the chips smoothly**:
   - Warm **~80–120 g** of the milk until hot but not boiling (microwave is fine).
   - Add the **35 g chips** and let sit 30–60 seconds, then stir until fully melted.
2. Add the melted chocolate + remaining milk + cream + whey + sugar (if using) + xanthan + salt (+ vanilla if using).
3. Blend until completely smooth (no whey clumps).
4. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint.
5. Freeze for 24 hours.
6. Spin on **Lite Ice Cream**.
7. If powdery/crumbly, add **10–15 g milk** and **Re-Spin**.

## Notes / tweaks

- If you want **less rich**, use **45 g cream** and **395 g milk** instead.
- If you want it **darker**, increase chips to **45 g** (you may not need any added sugar).
- If you want a **“Frosty” / milkshake vibe**, keep chips at **30–35 g** and add a little extra sugar/sweetener to taste.
