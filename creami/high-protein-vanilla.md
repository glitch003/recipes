# High Protein Vanilla Creami

## Ingredients (grams)

- 220 g milk (2% recommended; adjust as needed)
- 190 g plain whole-milk yogurt
- 30 g heavy whipping cream
- 40 g unflavored whey protein powder
- 35 g sugar (or sweetener equivalent, to taste)
- 3 g vanilla extract
- 0.8 g xanthan gum
- 1 g salt (tiny pinch, but important)

## Nutrition (estimated)

- **Per pint**: **~575 kcal** — **Fat ~22 g**, **Carbs ~48 g**, **Protein ~48 g**
- **Per 1/2 pint**: **~288 kcal** — **Fat ~11 g**, **Carbs ~24 g**, **Protein ~24 g**
- **Assumptions**: Milk modeled as **2%**; yogurt modeled as plain **whole-milk yogurt**; heavy cream modeled as standard heavy whipping cream; whey modeled as ~**80% protein** by weight; sweetener assumed to be sugar (if using non-sugar sweetener, calories/carbs will be lower).

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients and blend until completely smooth (no whey clumps).
2. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint.
3. Freeze for 24 hours.
4. Spin on **Lite Ice Cream**.
5. If powdery/crumbly, add **10–15 g milk** and **Re-Spin**.

# Tasting notes

Just tastes like vanilla yogurt. Not great. Would not make again.
