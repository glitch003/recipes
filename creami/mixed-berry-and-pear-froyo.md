# Mixed Berry and Pear Froyo

## Ingredients (grams)

- 110 g mixed berries
- 40 g fresh pear (skin off)
- 170 g plain Greek yogurt (Fage-style, 0–5%)
- 90 g pear juice
- 35 g honey
- 1.2 g xanthan gum
- 3 g lemon juice (~½ tsp, brightens fruit)
- 1 g salt (tiny pinch, but important)
- 50 g half and half

## Nutrition (estimated)

- **Per pint**: **450 kcal** — **Fat 9.9 g**, **Carbs 69.6 g**, **Protein 22.9 g**, **Fiber 6.7 g**, **Sugar 60.8 g**, **Sodium ~393 mg**
- **Per 1/2 pint**: **225 kcal** — **Fat 5.0 g**, **Carbs 34.8 g**, **Protein 11.4 g**, **Fiber 3.4 g**, **Sugar 30.4 g**, **Sodium ~196 mg**
- **Assumptions**: Greek yogurt modeled as plain **2%**; pear juice modeled as unsweetened pear juice; sodium only includes the added salt (yogurt will add more).

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients and blend until smooth.
2. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint.
3. Freeze for 24 hours.
4. Spin on the "Frozen Yogurt" setting.
5. If powdery, add 15g Pear Juice and Re-spin.

# Tasting notes

- Not very flavorful. Bianca thinks it's too tart. I like it but it's just decent froyo. Prob too much pear. Maybe more berries instead but I ran out.
