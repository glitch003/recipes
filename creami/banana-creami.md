# Banana Creami (Minimal & Creamy)

## Ingredients (all in grams)

- Banana (kinda ripe) — **126 g**
- Fage Greek yogurt — **151 g**
- Whole milk — **120 g**
- Honey — **25 g**
- Vanilla extract — **2 g**
- Xanthan gum — **0.4 g**

### Optional mix-in

- Extra dark chocolate chips — **25 g**

**Tasting notes:** needs salt, gwen wants peanut butter

## Instructions (Ninja Creami)

1. **Blend**
   - Blend all base ingredients until completely smooth.
   - Taste and adjust sweetness *before freezing* if needed.

2. **Freeze**
   - Pour into Ninja Creami pint.
   - Freeze flat and uncovered for **12–24 hours**, until solid.

3. **Spin**
   - Run **Lite Ice Cream** mode.
   - If crumbly, add **10–15 g whole milk** and **Re-Spin**.

4. **Mix-In (optional)**
   - Add chocolate chips and run **Mix-In** mode.

## Texture Tweaks (Optional)

- **Richer / cheesecake-like:** replace **30 g** milk with **30 g** half-and-half  
- **Less tangy:** reduce yogurt to **120 g**, add **30 g** milk  
- **Pear-banana:** replace **40 g** milk with **40 g** pear juice

Enjoy a smooth, scoopable, banana-forward Creami 🍦
