# Chocolate Oreo Ice Cream Creami

## Ingredients (grams)

- 240 g milk (whole recommended)
- 120 g heavy whipping cream
- 50 g sugar (or sweetener equivalent, to taste)
- 30 g cream cheese, softened
- 25 g unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 g vanilla extract
- 0.8 g xanthan gum
- 1 g salt (tiny pinch, but important)
- 60 g Oreo cookies, roughly crushed (~6 cookies)

## Nutrition (estimated)

- **Per pint**: **~1,000 kcal** — **Fat ~52 g**, **Carbs ~118 g**, **Protein ~17 g**
- **Per 1/2 pint**: **~500 kcal** — **Fat ~26 g**, **Carbs ~59 g**, **Protein ~8.5 g**
- **Assumptions**: Whole milk; standard heavy whipping cream; regular Oreo cookies (~53 kcal per cookie); full-fat cream cheese; unsweetened cocoa powder (~12 kcal per 5 g).

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, heavy cream, sugar, cream cheese, cocoa powder, vanilla, xanthan gum, and salt. Blend until completely smooth (make sure the cocoa powder is fully incorporated).
2. Fold in the crushed Oreo pieces by hand (keep them chunky for texture).
3. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint.
4. Freeze for 24 hours.
5. Spin on **Ice Cream**.
6. If powdery/crumbly, add **10–15 g milk** and **Re-Spin**.

## Tips

- For extra Oreo flavor, finely crush 2 additional cookies into the base before blending so they dissolve into the mix, then fold in the remaining chunks.
- Stuffed or Double Stuf Oreos work great if you want more filling richness.
- A tablespoon of instant espresso powder (~2 g) enhances the chocolate flavor without making it taste like coffee.
