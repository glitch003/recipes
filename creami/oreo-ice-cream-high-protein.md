# Oreo Ice Cream Creami (High Protein)

## Ingredients (grams)

- 200 g Greek yogurt (0% fat)
- 100 g milk (whole or 2%)
- 30 g unflavored whey protein powder
- 20 g cream cheese, softened
- 15 g sugar (or sweetener equivalent, to taste)
- 5 g vanilla extract
- 0.8 g xanthan gum
- 1 g salt
- 40 g Oreo cookies, roughly crushed (~4 cookies)

## Nutrition (estimated)

- **Per pint**: **~610 kcal** — **Fat ~18 g**, **Carbs ~60 g**, **Protein ~49 g**
- **Per 1/2 pint**: **~305 kcal** — **Fat ~9 g**, **Carbs ~30 g**, **Protein ~25 g**
- **Assumptions**: 0% Greek yogurt; whole milk; 24g protein per 30g scoop; full-fat cream cheese; standard Oreos (~47 kcal each).

## Instructions

1. Combine Greek yogurt, milk, protein powder, cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, xanthan gum, and salt. Blend until completely smooth — no lumps.
2. Fold in the crushed Oreo pieces by hand (keep them chunky for texture).
3. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint.
4. Freeze for 24 hours.
5. Spin on **Ice Cream**.
6. If powdery/crumbly (likely with yogurt-based bases), add **15–20 g milk** and **Re-Spin**.

## Tips

- Unflavored whey has no sweetness or flavor of its own — the extra vanilla and Oreos carry it. Taste the base before freezing and adjust sugar if needed.
- Greek yogurt bases tend to spin crumbly — don't skip the Re-Spin with extra milk.
- For even more protein (~55 g/pint), swap the cream cheese for 30 g cottage cheese blended smooth.
- Finely crush 1–2 extra cookies into the base before blending to get Oreo flavor throughout, then fold in chunks.
